# DGDA-Employment-Ratio
Overview: This personal project highlights the employment ratio in the Kingdom, specifically in Diriyah Gate Development Authority's based on saudization and gender
Years in focus: 2022-2025

<img width="1135" height="388" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ded1405d-69bd-4c36-8ea9-936d3a04b9fe" />

Conclusion: 
- DGDA consistently prioritized workforce nationalization, maintaining an average 96% Saudization rate between 2022 and 2025.
- The workforce structure reflects support for gender inclusion, with a stable 56% male to 44% female employee ratio across the four-year period.
- The employment trends demonstrate DGDA’s commitment to developing local talent and increasing female participation in the workforce.
- These outcomes align with the objectives of Saudi Vision 2030, supporting national employment growth, women’s empowerment, and a diverse, sustainable workforce.
